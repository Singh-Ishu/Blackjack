Blackjack



How to Play Blackjack?

Press play
The dealer deals one card to you and one to itself, without showing you its card
The dealer then deals another hand to both you and itself, Although this time its card is visible
You may stay or hit
Once you are done with the hits the dealer reveals its hand
If your hand is greater than the dealer you win
If your hand is 21, you win
If you and the dealer both have a hand of 21, it's a tie
If your hand at any time reaches over 21 you lose
